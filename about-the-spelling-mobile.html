Learn Erzya

The Romanization of the Erzya language used in this dictionary is loosely based on the proposal used in the book by linguists Ksenija Djordjević and Jean-Léo Léonard, Parlons mordve (erzya et mokša).

❗ The transliteration tool may have some shortcomings due to the limitations of the Cyrillic script. It is recommended to use combinations like ньа, дьа, дьу, etc., in compound words such as ведьава (ведь+ава), иньазор (инь+азор), зьаро (зь+аро), etc.

❗ Moreover, the tool supports only Sentence case and lower case. UPPER CASE will not work correctly!
